Who can invest through Steady Growth?
Any resident or NRI/OCI adult with valid KYC can invest. I work best with people focused on long-term goals — not short-term speculation or stock tips.
Is there a minimum investment required?
There is no fixed minimum. SIPs can start as low as ₹500, but most long-term structured plans typically begin from ₹2,000–₹10,000+ monthly based on goals.
Do you offer stock tips or trading calls?
No. I strictly follow a goal-based, suitability-driven investing approach — not trading, speculation or timing the market.
What is the onboarding process?
You share your details → short discussion → risk profile & goals → suitable strategy → digital onboarding via NJ Wealth platform.
What documents are required?
PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, photo & signature. For NRIs: passport, visa details, FATCA & NRE/NRO account.
Are mutual funds guaranteed?
No. Mutual funds are market-linked. Returns depend on time horizon, fund choice & discipline — not predictions.
How do you manage portfolio risk?
Goal matching, asset allocation, SIP approach, suitable categories, and periodic review. Safety is prioritised over excitement.
Can NRIs invest through you?
Yes — most NRIs can invest using NRE/NRO accounts depending on their country. Some country restrictions apply due to FATCA rules.
How are withdrawals handled?
Withdrawals go to the linked NRE/NRO bank account as per the scheme. Repatriation rules apply for NRE-based investments.
Do you charge advisory fees?
I operate as a Mutual Fund Distributor and receive standard regulated commissions from AMCs. No hidden fees are charged to clients.
Direct plan vs regular plan?
Direct plans have no distributor commission, while regular plans include service-based commissions. Clients choose what suits them best.
