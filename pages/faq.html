Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions from new investors.

Do you guarantee any returns?
No. I do not, and cannot, guarantee any returns. Mutual Fund investments are subject to market risks. My role is to help you invest wisely and stay disciplined, not to predict or promise returns.
How do I start investing with you?
You can reach out via WhatsApp or the contact form. We’ll schedule an initial call to understand your goals, after which I will guide you on onboarding and execution through NJ Wealth.
Will I get direct plan mutual funds?
I facilitate investments in regular plans which include distributor commissions. If you prefer direct plans without distributor involvement, you may invest directly with AMCs or platforms that offer direct plans.
Do you handle taxation or financial planning?
I can share general information on how different investments are taxed, but I do not provide tax or legal advice. For complex taxation or financial planning needs, please consult a qualified CA or financial planner.
Which platform do you use for execution?
All investments are executed digitally through the NJ Wealth platform, backed by secure infrastructure, documentation, and tracking.
What type of clients do you work with?
I primarily work with working professionals, NRIs, HNIs, business owners, and families focused on long-term wealth creation—not short-term speculation.
