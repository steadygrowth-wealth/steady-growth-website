How We Work Together

A structured, disciplined, and transparent approach—so you always know what’s happening with your investments.

1. Intro call & understanding your goals

We discuss your goals, current investments, time horizon and comfort with risk over a call or meeting.

2. Risk profile & plan outline

Based on your risk profile, I outline a suitable asset mix and appropriate mutual fund categories and schemes.

3. Execution via NJ Wealth

Investments are executed digitally on the NJ Wealth platform for convenience, documentation and tracking.

4. Review & course-correction

We review periodically to ensure your investments remain aligned with goals and update the plan when needed.
