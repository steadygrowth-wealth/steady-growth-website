What Are SEBI & AMFI?

SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) is India’s securities market regulator that creates the framework under which mutual funds operate.

AMFI (Association of Mutual Funds in India) maintains information related to mutual fund distributors and industry standards.

What Is an ARN?

ARN (AMFI Registration Number) is assigned to registered mutual fund distributors as part of the formal distributor registration process.

Investors can independently look up an ARN on AMFI’s public portal to confirm basic registration details.

Why Should You Verify?

When it comes to finances, credibility and transparency are extremely important. Verification helps ensure that you are interacting with an officially registered distributor following the mutual fund ecosystem’s regulatory framework.

How to Verify My ARN

You will be redirected to the official AMFI website where you can manually search and verify distributor registration details.
